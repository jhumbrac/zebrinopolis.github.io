Zebrinopolis

One of a Kind Plush Creations

Hi, I make plushies. Each plush is made by hand, modeled from concept sketches. Zebrinopolis explores what creatures could be on distant planets, in realities parallel to our own.

Some of the creations look similar to animals from our planet, while others appear to be from different universes. You will find creatures such as:

  • Buffalo
  • Interdimensional Worms
  • Otherworldly Shaman
  • Seahorses
  • Space Deer
